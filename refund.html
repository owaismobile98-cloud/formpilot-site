Refund Policy

Effective: 5 July 2026.

14-day money-back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with FormPilot Pro, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase. For a renewal charge, contact us within 14 days of that charge.

How to request a refund

Email us or open an issue at our support page with the email used at checkout. Refunds are issued to your original payment method by our Merchant of Record, Paddle.com, typically within 5–10 business days.

Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewals; your Pro access continues until the end of the current paid period. Cancelling does not by itself trigger a refund of the current period outside the 14-day window.

Exceptions

Refunds may be declined where there is evidence of abuse (e.g. repeated refund requests) or where required otherwise by law.
