Understands your documents

Drop in a CV, ID card or any PDF/Word/image. On-device OCR + AI extract your details — education, every job with dates, contacts — into a structured private vault.

Multi-step job applications, repeating work-history blocks, date-format quirks — FormPilot maps the right value to the right field and self-heals rejected formats.
