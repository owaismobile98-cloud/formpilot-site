Privacy Policy

Effective: 5 July 2026 · Applies to the FormPilot browser extension.

Summary: FormPilot is built so your personal data never leaves your device. We run no servers for your data, require no account, and collect no analytics about you or your documents. Everything below follows from that architecture.

1. What data FormPilot handles

FormPilot helps you store personal information (such as your name, contact details, education, work history, and documents like a CV) and uses it to fill web forms you choose to fill. All of this data is processed and stored locally in your browser (extension local storage / IndexedDB) on your own device.

2. What we collect on our servers

Nothing. FormPilot has no backend for your personal data. We cannot see, receive, sell, or share your information, because it is never transmitted to us.

3. On-device AI

Document reading (OCR) and AI extraction run inside your browser using bundled, on-device models. These features work offline and do not send your documents anywhere.

4. Optional cloud features (off by default)

If you explicitly enable an optional cloud AI feature (for example, a "max intelligence" mode in a paid tier or a provider key you configure yourself), the text needed for that specific action is sent to the AI provider you chose, under their terms. This is opt-in, clearly labelled in the interface, and never enabled silently. Disable it at any time to return to fully on-device operation.

5. Permissions the extension uses

  • Access to the page you're viewing — to detect form fields and fill them when you ask.
  • Local storage — to keep your profile and documents on your device.

FormPilot's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Chrome Web Store Limited Use requirements, including the prohibition on transferring or selling user data.

6. Payments

Paid plans, when available, are processed by a third-party merchant of record (such as Paddle). Your payment details go to that processor, not to FormPilot; we receive only a license status.

7. Your control

  • Export or delete all locally stored data at any time from the extension's Profile tab ("Export" / "Reset all").
  • Uninstalling the extension removes its local data per your browser's standard behavior.

8. Children

FormPilot is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly handle their data.

9. Changes

We will update this page when the policy changes and note the new effective date above. Material changes will be highlighted in the extension's release notes.

10. Contact

Questions: open an issue at github.com/owaismobile98-cloud/formpilot-site.
